What is Hand Analysis?

One of the most powerful ways we train players is through hand analysis, where our pros break down real poker hands step-by-step and explain the thinking behind every decision at each stage of the hand. By reviewing situations this way, participants learn how professionals evaluate information, calculate probabilities, and identify optimal strategies. Studying hands in detail helps players recognize mistakes, refine their decision-making, and improve their ability to analyze complex situations in the future.

For BCG professionals, this format is especially effective because it mirrors the analytical frameworks consultants use every day — examining a scenario, evaluating available data, and determining the most strategic course of action.
